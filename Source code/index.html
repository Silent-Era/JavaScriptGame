Welcome to Triforce!

 

 

 

How to Play:

 

Press: ↑ to Jump!

 

Press: ← to Move Left!

 

Press: → to Move Right!

 

Press: ↓ to Jump Down from Ceiling!

 

Press: Space to Jump on the Ceiling!

 

 

Note: The Goal is to avoid the obstacles!
